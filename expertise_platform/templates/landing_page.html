Welcome to the Expertise Sharing Platform

Connecting Academicians and Researchers for Innovation and Growth

Project Overview

This decentralized platform facilitates knowledge and expertise sharing among academicians and researchers. It includes profiles for institutions and staff, enhancing innovation within the academic community by providing access to a larger pool of experts for specific projects.

Key Features

Institution Section

View and manage institution profiles, post requirements, and search for specific skills or keywords.

Staff Section

Create and manage staff profiles, link certifications, and indicate project enrollment. Search for institutions or specific requirements.

Integration

Staff can browse requirements, participate in projects, and view profiles for direct contact.

Non-functional Requirements

The platform is designed for high performance, scalability, security, and usability across all major browsers and mobile devices.
